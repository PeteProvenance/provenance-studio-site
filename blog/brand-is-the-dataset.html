Brand is the dataset

Globally, trillions has been invested in making financial data active and queryable. That has never been possible for brand. It is now.

The strategy, discovery, articulation and codification of brand work is precisely the work of producing model training data. Brand is the dataset. We just never called it that.

Provenance turns that dataset into a sovereign brand intelligence: a model that knows your brand and only your brand, running on hardware you own, that never leaves your building and compounds in value over time.
